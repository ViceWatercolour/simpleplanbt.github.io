Project War

This is a project the Simple Plan Build Team started. The point of it is so create a War Wars version of every map that is on Overcast Network's rotations.

Select your Server's Rotation

After selecting the server's rotation you would like to see, you will be presented with the list of War Wars maps.

The number next to the server's shows the amount of the maps that are under that category.
